Favorite Projects

"Meat Pies! Get Your Meat Pies!"

I went back to live in Sydney after studying abroad there and pitched The Washington Post to run an article about Australian meat pies. They accepted. I wrote, edited, and provided photos for a Travel section piece, my favorite byline ever.

A Form Field Overhaul

One of my favorite byproducts of a project to overhaul an online account opening experience: a new form field strategy! Because form fields are part of the conversation too. (Go on, ask me about my favorite.)

An Ecommerce Homepage Redesign

We transformed our homepage to give customers what they want, properly tell our brand story, and sell more phones. I made sure every space had a purpose and was relevant for our 32 international sites.

A Magazine Redesign

I was the acting managing editor during the redesign of a prominent home-building publication that became a finalist in the best redesign category for the Jesse H. Neal Award, the B2B equivalent to a Pulitzer.

In-the-Works: A Children's Book About an Island Chicken

Lucy's a determined hen who can't get up the tree to roost with her friends and family. Will she ever? I'm currently incorporating feedback from parents and their kids and am on the lookout for an illustrator.
